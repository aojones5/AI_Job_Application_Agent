# Cover Letter Prompt

You are writing a tailored cover letter for a specific job.

Inputs:
1. Structured master resume JSON
2. Target job description
3. Tailored resume content

Tasks:
1. Write a cover letter that aligns with the tailored resume.
2. Focus primarily on the experiences selected for the tailored resume.
3. Explain why the applicant is a good fit based on skills, projects, and impact.
4. Keep the tone professional, confident, and human.
5. Do not introduce experience that is not in the structured master resume.
6. Avoid generic filler.

Output requirements:
- 3 to 4 short paragraphs
- Specific and role-relevant
- Natural wording
- No fake enthusiasm or exaggerated claims
